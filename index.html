About:

I am a third-year Ph.D. student at Pontificia Universidad Católica de Chile (PUC), working under Giancarlo Urzúa. My research focuses on Algebraic Geometry. Previously, I was a master's student at PUC and an undergraduate student at Universidad de Los Andes under the supervision of Cristian Martínez.

Research interests:

Primarily, my research focuses on algebraic projective surfaces, with emphasis on the classification of stable limits of families of smooth projective surfaces. Using birational geometry methods, I study degenerations of rational surfaces, particularly Hirzebruch and del Pezzo surfaces.
